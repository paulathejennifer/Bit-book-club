    Women in relationship and work

    Today, no profession is meant to be only for a man or a woman. A Girl can do any job, even though some
    work is not advisable, as it can cause damage to her physical body.




    Women in the past were not allowed to work, possess any property, or learn. The only thing they
    could do was get married and give birth. This has damaged the psychology and health of women: it make
    them think they cannot do anything other than raise children. Such an attitude affected the family, country, and the world, because they have much to contribute and solve problems. So I believe the whole community should be supportive and do whatever it takes for women to get an education and access to health service.

    Men are also part of the community, so they have to help encourage women. But from my experience, most of them including Abel want a girl just to be in her home, and take care of the family. They do not have a desire for their girl to be independent: they do not choose a free, confident girl who has the potential to do all by herself. They want to get married to a girl who can make time for her family.

    I understand their wish, and I want to emphasize that, an educated woman can handle every responsibility of work and family.
